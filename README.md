# ⚡ Energy Insights: Consumption Analytics Dashboard

> Turning half-hourly energy readings into site-level strategic decisions.

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes energy consumption data across multiple sites and regions, uncovering demand patterns, consumption inefficiencies, and opportunities for energy optimization. The dataset contains readings at **half-hour intervals** across different sites — requiring careful data consolidation before meaningful analysis could begin.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Energy costs represent a significant operational expense for multi-site organizations. Without visibility into consumption patterns by site, region, and time, leadership cannot make informed decisions about energy allocation, efficiency investments, or peak demand management.

---

## 🔧 Approach & Methodology

| Step | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Data Consolidation | Unified data from multiple Excel and CSV sources into one consistent dataset |
| Data Modeling | Built robust relational model with optimized table relationships |
| DAX Measures | Calculated consumption KPIs, demand trends, and efficiency ratios |
| Dashboard Design | Designed interactive dashboards segmented by time, region, and site |

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- 📊 **Demand Variation**: Significant consumption differences identified across sites and regions
- ⏰ **Time Patterns**: Peak demand hours vary by site type — enabling targeted load management
- 🌍 **Regional Efficiency**: Clear performance gaps between high and low efficiency regions
- 💡 **Optimization Opportunities**: Data surfaced specific sites with disproportionate consumption relative to output
